About Us

We are Team Ideologics. We are a group of passionate students from Marian Engineering College, CSE department. We try to provide people with new creative ideas and works that makes their life easier.

GamesHub is our latest endeavour. We aim to provide gamers with a new platform from where they can download & update new games, stream their gameplay and achieve new height. We also provide an option for users to register for tournaments that are conducted around the world.
